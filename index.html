Swarmwise Methods for Decentralized Action

Become a volunteer.

This swarm is for people who believe in free culture and non-commercial sharing.

Volunteers include remixers, cover singers, fan editors, fan dubbers, and anyone who shares culture out of passion rather than profit.

There are no roles to assign and no permission to ask. Each person decides how to contribute and takes initiative in their own way.

If you support digital freedom and free culture — values shared by many in the Pirate Party — you are likely among the right people here.
