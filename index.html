Swarm of Free Culture

Inspired by Rick Falkvinge’s Swarmwise book

We work to make non-profit sharing of culture legal worldwide because creativity and culture are human rights.

People share what they love. Criminalizing that pushes people away.

Culture doesn’t survive behind locks.

Creativity should not require permission. If you care about creativity, you belong here.

No permission — everyone does what they think is right. Mistakes welcome. Everyone welcome.
