This swarm is for anyone who believes in free culture, copyright reform, and digital freedom. Volunteers amplify creators’ voices and protect creative rights online.

Volunteers include remixers, cover singers, fan editors, fan dubbers, and anyone sharing culture out of passion rather than profit.

No roles to assign, no permissions to ask — each person decides how to contribute and takes initiative in their own way.
